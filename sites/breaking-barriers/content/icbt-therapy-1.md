## What is ICBT? {#what-is-icbt}

ICBT takes the proven science of ABA and brings it into today’s world—where children deserve therapy that is playful, collaborative, and neuro-affirming.

### It means:

{{< cols min="15rem" gap="1.5rem" v="start" stagger="false" >}}

{{< col card="true" y="start" >}}
### Collaborative

Your child’s team (speech, OT, behavior, and you) works together, not in silos.
{{< /col >}}

{{< col card="true" y="start" >}}
### Intensive

Progress happens faster when therapy is consistent and focused.
{{< /col >}}

{{< col card="true" y="start" >}}
### Playful

Sessions look like play, but every moment is designed for growth.
{{< /col >}}

{{< col card="true" y="start" >}}
### Empowering

Parents are trained partners, so progress continues at home.
{{< /col >}}

{{< /cols >}}
