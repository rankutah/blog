## Meet Josh and Jenn Taylor

#### Founders and Owners of Breaking Barriers Therapy Services

Our journey began on the frontlines of parenthood and ignited our passion and purpose, shaping the very essence of who we are today.

It all started with our own personal struggle to access the vital services our child needed. Frustration, obstacles, and countless moments of despair marked the process of finding support difficult, but within those moments of adversity, a spark was lit. Our passion to ensure that no other family would have to endure what we had experienced grew.

Breaking Barriers Therapy Services was born out of our desire to be a beacon of hope and a source of exceptional care and community for families just like you.  Our goal goes beyond providing therapy services; it's about breaking down the barriers that hinder children and families from reaching their full potential.
