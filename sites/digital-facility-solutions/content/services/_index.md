## Digital Facility Binder

We transform legacy building plans (including large format sheets) into searchable, navigable digital facility binders—so your team can find the right information fast.

## Core Deliverables

- Structured PDF files
- Hyperlinked index / table of contents
- Page labels based on disciplines and drawing numbers
- File-level and content-level searchability
- Image skew/alignment correction where required

## Additional Services

- Document organization
- Quantity take-offs
- Redline conversions
- Multi-project consolidation
- CMMS integration support
