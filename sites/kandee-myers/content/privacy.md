# Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{< year >}}

This Privacy Policy explains how the Kandee Myers campaign (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you visit this website or submit a form.

## Information we collect

- **Information you provide** when you submit a form (for example: name, email, phone number, and the message you send).
- **Technical information** that may be logged automatically (for example: IP address, device/browser information, and pages visited) for security and performance.
- **Cookies / local storage** used for basic site functionality and preferences.

## How we use information

- Respond to messages and requests.
- Coordinate volunteer activity and campaign communications.
- Improve site performance, reliability, and user experience.
- Protect against abuse, spam, and security incidents.

We do not sell your personal information.

## Sharing information

We may share information with service providers that help us operate the site (for example: hosting, spam prevention, and analytics). These providers are only permitted to use information to provide services to us.

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of the campaign and site visitors.

## Cookies, analytics, and spam prevention

This site may use:

- **Spam prevention** tools on forms.
- **Analytics** to understand general site usage and improve the site.
- **Preference storage** (for example: theme preference and motion/animation settings) stored in your browser.

You can control cookies and local storage through your browser settings.

## Your choices

- You can choose not to submit forms.
- If you have submitted information and want it updated or deleted, contact us via the [Contact](/contact) page.

## Data retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted on this page.

## Contact

Questions about privacy? Please use the [Contact](/contact) page.
