# Voter Info

Election details can change. This page prioritizes **official Utah election resources** so it stays accurate.

## Key date

- **General Election:** Tuesday, November 3, 2026

Not sure what district you’re in? Start here: [Find Your District (map)](/district-map)

## Confirm your registration and districts (official links)

- Check voter registration status: https://votesearch.utah.gov/
- Register to vote / update registration: https://secure.utah.gov/voterreg/
- Find your districts / district map tools: https://le.utah.gov/GIS/findDistrict.jsp
- Utah election info hub (deadlines, early voting, where to vote): https://vote.utah.gov/

## Helpful checklist

- Confirm you’re registered and your address is current.
- Check your districts (they can change after redistricting).
- Watch this page for early voting and vote-by-mail details as the election gets closer.
