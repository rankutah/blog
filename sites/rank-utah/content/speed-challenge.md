# Website Speed Challenge


## Are you losing leads?

If your website takes **longer than 5 seconds to load, you are likely losing over 50% of your mobile leads**.

## Test Your Website

1. Open <a href="https://pagespeed.web.dev/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">Google PageSpeed Insights</a>
2. Enter your website
3. Wait about 20 seconds for the report
4. Compare your results to clearpresence.io
