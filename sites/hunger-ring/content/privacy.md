# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how **Suggls** (“we”, “us”) collects and uses information in connection with the **Hunger Ring** website and product.

## Information we collect

We may collect:

- Information you provide when you contact us (such as your name, email address, and message)
- Information you provide when you place an order (such as shipping details)
- Basic technical information (such as browser type and approximate location) that helps us keep the site running and understand usage

## How we use information

We use information to:

- Respond to messages and support requests
- Process orders, fulfill shipments, and handle returns
- Improve our website and customer experience
- Prevent fraud and protect the security of our services

## Sharing

We don’t sell your personal information.

We may share information with service providers who help us run the business (for example: web hosting, analytics, email, payment processing, and shipping). They are authorized to use information only as needed to provide their services.

## Data retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described above, including to comply with legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.
